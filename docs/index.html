The Simple, Focused Pomodoro Timer

A minimalist Pomodoro timer for the macOS menu bar. Stay on task, take meaningful breaks, and keep your session history synced effortlessly.

Features

Simple & Clean

Lives in your menu bar, staying out of your way until you need it.

Cloud-Synced Stats

Your session history is automatically saved to provide insights into your work patterns.

Configurable Timers

Easily adjust the duration for your focus, short break, and long break intervals.

Keyboard First

Start, pause, and tag your sessions instantly with keyboard shortcuts.

Session Tagging

Tag your focus sessions to keep track of what you're working on.

Instant Notifications

Get a clear notification when it's time to switch tasks.

Track Your Progress

Review your session history with a clean and simple stats view. See your total focus time, session counts, and streaks to understand your work patterns and stay motivated.

Customize Your Flow

Easily adjust the duration for your focus, short break, and long break intervals. The settings are simple to configure and update instantly.
